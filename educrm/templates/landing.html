Process

A Clear Loop from Classroom to Leadership

EduVoice closes the feedback gap between what happens in class and what school leaders can act on.

1

Admin Opens Review Period

The school admin activates a semester's review window. Students can only submit within this controlled period.

2

Students Submit Reviews

Each student rates their learning experience per subject across five CBC-aligned dimensions — anonymously.

3

Teachers See Aggregated Insights

Teachers receive anonymous, averaged feedback — no individual identities, just clear signals for improvement.

4

Parents Track Their Child

Parents see their child's subject ratings, soft skill self-ratings, and can leave an encouraging note.

5

Admin Reviews CRM Analytics

School leadership gets participation rates, subject enjoyment rankings, and teacher clarity scores — private to admin.

Platform Principles

Designed Around Trust & Privacy

Every design decision protects the people using it — especially children.

🔒

Anonymity Enforced at Code Level

Teachers only see aggregated averages. Student identities are never shown to teachers — ever. This is enforced in the view layer, not just by policy.

🏫

Multi-Tenant from Day One

Each school's data is fully isolated. Adding a new school is a single record — no code changes. Built for SaaS deployment across many schools.

🗓️

Admin-Controlled Review Periods

Students can only submit reviews when a school admin opens the semester window. No unsolicited collection of student data.

📊

CRM Analytics for Leadership

School admins see participation rates, subject enjoyment trends, and teacher clarity ratings — private data that never reaches teachers or parents.

💚

Positive, CBC-Framed Questions

Reviews ask "what did you love?" and "what made you curious?" — not complaint forms. Encouraging reflection, not judgment.

📱

Mobile-Ready Interface

Fully responsive with a mobile bottom navigation bar. Students can submit reviews from any device — no app download required.
