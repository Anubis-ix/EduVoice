2. Description of Service

EduVoice Kenya is a multi-role, multi-tenant school feedback and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform designed for Kenyan schools operating under the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC). The Platform provides:

  • Anonymous end-of-term subject review tools for students (Grades 1–9)
  • Aggregated, anonymous feedback dashboards for teachers
  • Per-child academic and soft skill reports for parents and guardians
  • School-wide participation and analytics dashboards for school administrators
  • Semester management tools for review period control
The Platform is scoped per school. Each school's data is isolated and inaccessible to other schools on the Platform.
